# Wasabi 2.0 Destination Transaction

Wasabi 2.0 destination transactions spend the outputs of Wasabi 2.0 mixing transactions. To qualify as a destination transaction, the source of at least one of its inputs must be a Wasabi 2.0 mixing transaction.
