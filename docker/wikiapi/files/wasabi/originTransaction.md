# Wasabi 2.0 Origin Transaction

Wasabi 2.0 origin transactions create outputs which are used by Wasabi 2.0 mixing transactions. To qualify as a Wasabi 2.0 origin transaction, at least one of its outputs must be spent by a Wasabi 2.0 mixing transaction.

Compared to other CoinJoin implementations, Wasabi 2.0 does not require to split the funds in predefined denominations before mixing. Instead, [denominated](wasabi/denominations.md) outputs are only created via mixing transactions.
